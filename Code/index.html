If you're like me, you read. A lot! Especially online. But there's so much interesting stuff out there. How does one even decide what to read at any give time?

Rocketship is a tool which makes it much easier for you to read your favourite pieces of content on the web. And it's super simple to use.

Step 1 - Just click on the plus button on the bottom right.

Step 2 - Add links of the blogs or publications that you follow.

Step 3 - When you have a few minutes to read, simply click on the 'Let's Go button'

Everytime you click 'Let's Go', Rocketship takes you to a randomly selected link from your list, thus saving you from decision paralysis.

Happy Reading!
